WeSound

The ultimate tool for managing your Wii mod's soundtrack.

Easily convert, save, and patch your Wii music files.

WeSound supports .brstm conversion and editing, .brsar patching, and even BAHP editing, all in the same app.

Make "final lap" variants with one button.

Choose the speed, and if you want loops and BAHPs automatically adjusted.

WeSound can detect if there is a fast variant for your selected track!

Custom NSMBW BAHPs are simple to implement.

With WeSound's built-in BAHP editor, you can easily add/remove BAHPs directly to .brstm matching .brseq chunks, all in an intuitive UI.

Using framework from the original BAHP Editor, you can even visualize BAHP points on an associated .brstm.

Most importantly, custom themes!

Pick from WeSound's light or dark theme, or a custom theme for the NSMBW cast!

Support for custom themes coming soon.
